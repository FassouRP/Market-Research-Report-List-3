Mid-oleic oil has a medium oleic acid content and is ideal for frying and roasting. High-oleic oil has the highest oleic acid content and is suitable for high-temperature cooking and industrial use. Each market type offers different culinary and industrial applications based on its oleic acid content.</p></p>
