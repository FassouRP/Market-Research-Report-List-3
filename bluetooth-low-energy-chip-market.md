<p><p>The Bluetooth Low Energy chip market consists of two main types: Enhanced Data Rate (EDR) and High Speed/Alternate MAC/PHY (HS/AMP). EDR allows for faster data transfer rates, making it ideal for applications requiring quick and efficient communication. On the other hand, HS/AMP is designed for applications that require high-speed data exchange, such as streaming audio or video. Both types cater to different market needs, offering improved performance and reliability in various Bluetooth Low Energy devices.</p></p>
