<p><p>Dialyzer reprocessing systems are available in two main types: semi-automatic and fully automatic. Semi-automatic systems require some manual intervention from healthcare personnel, such as connecting and disconnecting the dialyzer. On the other hand, fully automatic systems operate without the need for manual input, performing all steps of the reprocessing cycle automatically. Both types offer benefits in terms of efficiency, ease of use, and consistency in sterilizing dialyzers for reuse in dialysis procedures.</p></p>
