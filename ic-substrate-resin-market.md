<p><strong>The IC Substrate Resin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>BT Resin</li><li>ABF Resin</li><li>BT Resin Alternative</li><li>Build-up Film Resin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>IC substrate resin market consists of various types such as BT resin, ABF resin, BT resin alternative, and Build-up Film resin. BT resin is widely used for its high thermal conductivity and reliability. ABF resin is preferred for its low dielectric constant and ease of processing. BT resin alternative offers similar properties to BT resin but at a lower cost. Build-up Film resin is known for its high-density interconnection capabilities. Each type of resin caters to different needs in the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
