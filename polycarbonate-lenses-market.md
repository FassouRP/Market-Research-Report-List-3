<p><p>Polycarbonate lenses are available in various types to cater to different vision needs. Single vision lenses are used to correct vision at a single distance, while bifocal lenses have two distinct optical powers for close-up and distance vision. Trifocal lenses provide three distinct focal points for near, intermediate, and distance vision. Progressive lenses offer a seamless transition between different focal points, providing a more natural vision experience. Each type of lens is designed to address specific vision requirements and offer clear and comfortable vision correction.</p></p>
