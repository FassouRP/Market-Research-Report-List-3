<p><p>Fibre Reinforced Plastic (FRP) Pipes are classified into three main types - Glass Reinforced Plastic (GRP) Pipe, Glass Reinforced Epoxy (GRE) Pipe, and Glass Reinforced Vinyl Ester (GRV) Pipe. GRP Pipes are made with a polyester resin and glass fiber combination, offering good resistance to corrosion. GRE Pipes have epoxy resin and glass fiber composition, providing high strength and durability. GRV Pipes are manufactured using vinyl ester resin and glass fiber, offering superior chemical resistance. These FRP Pipes are widely used in various industries for their unique properties and long-lasting performance.</p></p>
