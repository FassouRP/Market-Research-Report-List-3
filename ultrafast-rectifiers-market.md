<p><p>Ultrafast rectifiers are available in different market types to suit various applications. Dual Common Anode rectifiers have two anodes connected together, providing a common connection for both diodes. Dual Common Cathode rectifiers have two cathodes connected together, providing a common connection for both diodes. Single rectifiers have only one diode, making them suitable for applications that require a single rectification process. Each market type offers different benefits and features, allowing for customization based on specific application requirements.</p></p>
