<p><p>Industrial multiwall paper bags come in various types to suit different applications. Sewn open mouth bags are closed at the top by sewing. Pasted open mouth bags have the top folded and pasted shut. Pasted valve bags have a valve for easy filling and closing. Pinch bottom bags have a flat bottom with a pinched closure. Self-opening satchel bags feature a pre-glued flap for easy filling and closure. Each type offers unique benefits and is used in different industries based on their specific needs.</p></p>
