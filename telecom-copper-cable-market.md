<p><p>The Telecom Copper Cable market can be categorized into two main types: Dry Core and Jelly-filled Core. Dry Core cables are filled with air and do not contain any gel or filling material, while Jelly-filled Core cables have a layer of jelly or filling material surrounding the copper conductor. Dry Core cables are typically used in dry environments where moisture is not a major concern, while Jelly-filled Core cables are preferred for outdoor installations or areas where moisture ingress is a potential issue.</p></p>
