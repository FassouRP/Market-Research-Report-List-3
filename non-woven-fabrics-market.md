<p><p>Non-woven fabrics market can be classified into three main types based on the production process: dry-laid type, spunmelt type, and wet-laid type. Dry-laid type non-woven fabrics are made by bonding fibers together through mechanical or chemical processes. Spunmelt type involves extruding molten polymer through spinnerets onto a conveyor to create a web of fibers. Wet-laid type non-woven fabrics are produced by suspending fibers in water and then depositing them onto a conveyor belt. Each type offers unique characteristics and applications in various industries.</p></p>
