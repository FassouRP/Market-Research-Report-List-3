<p><p>Digital switch-mode power supplies include two main market types: AC/DC and DC/DC. AC/DC power supplies convert alternating current (AC) from the main power grid into direct current (DC) for electronic devices. These are commonly used in household electronics and appliances. On the other hand, DC/DC power supplies convert one DC voltage level to another, commonly used in applications such as battery-powered devices, automotive electronics, and telecommunications equipment. Both types of power supplies offer efficiency, reliability, and versatility in various electronic applications.</p></p>
