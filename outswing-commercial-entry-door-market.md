<p><p>An Outswing Commercial Entry Door is a type of entry door commonly used in commercial buildings that opens outward. Firstly, the increasing number of commercial construction activities, such as office buildings, shopping malls, and retail stores, is creating a high demand for these doors. Outswing doors are perceived to be more secure, as the hinges and locks are located on the inside, making it difficult to tamper with from the outside.
