<p><p>Cleanroom laminar flow units are classified into two main types: vertical and horizontal. Vertical laminar flow units have a downward airflow that maintains a consistent unidirectional flow of air, creating a clean and controlled environment for sensitive processes such as pharmaceutical manufacturing. Horizontal laminar flow units have a horizontal airflow that is used for applications where the product needs to be protected from contamination, such as in electronics manufacturing. Both types of laminar flow units are essential for maintaining cleanroom environments in various industries.</p></p>
