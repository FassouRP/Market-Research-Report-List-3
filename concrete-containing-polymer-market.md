<p><p>Concrete containing polymer is a type of concrete that includes polymers to improve its properties. Polymer Concrete (PC) is a type where polymers are used as a binder instead of cement. Latex-modified Concrete (LMC) contains latex polymers to enhance flexibility and adhesion. Polymer-impregnated Concrete (PIC) involves impregnating polymers into the concrete to improve durability and strength. Other types of polymer concrete in the market include epoxy-modified concrete, polyester concrete, and vinyl ester concrete, each offering specific benefits in terms of chemical resistance, durability, and strength.</p></p>
