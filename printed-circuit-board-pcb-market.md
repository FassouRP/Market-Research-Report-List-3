<p><p>Printed Circuit Boards (PCBs) are electronic components used to mechanically support and electrically connect electronic components using conductive pathways etched from copper sheets laminated onto non-conductive substrates. The PCB market is segmented into Single Panel, Double Panel, and Multilayer Panel markets based on the number of conductive layers. Single Panel PCBs have one conductive layer, Double Panel PCBs have two, and Multilayer Panel PCBs have more than two, allowing for increased complexity and functionality in electronic devices.</p></p>
