<p><p>Commercial dosimetry services market includes various types of technologies for measuring radiation exposure. OSL technology utilizes aluminum oxide detectors, providing accurate and sensitive results. TLD technology uses lithium fluoride crystals for measuring dose levels with high precision. CR-39 technology involves using plastic detectors for recording neutron and alpha particle radiation. Each technology offers different features and benefits, catering to the diverse needs of industries such as healthcare, nuclear power, and manufacturing for ensuring safety and compliance with regulations.</p></p>
