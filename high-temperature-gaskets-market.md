<p><p>High temperature gaskets are essential in industries where extreme heat resistance is required. There are four main types of high temperature gaskets - metallic, semi-metallic, non-metallic, and UHT liquid gaskets. Metallic gaskets are made of materials like stainless steel and are highly durable. Semi-metallic gaskets combine metal and non-metal materials for improved flexibility. Non-metallic gaskets are made of materials like graphite or asbestos for high temperature resistance. UHT liquid gaskets are fluid-based sealants that can withstand extreme temperatures and pressures.</p></p>
