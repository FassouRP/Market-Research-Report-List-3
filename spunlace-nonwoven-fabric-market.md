<p><p>Parallel Lapped Spunlace Fabric and Cross Lapped Spunlace Fabric are two main types of spunlace nonwoven fabrics in the market. Parallel Lapped fabric is made by arranging fibers in a parallel direction, while Cross Lapped fabric involves crisscrossing fibers to create a more balanced structure. Parallel Lapped fabric is known for its strength and durability, while Cross Lapped fabric offers better absorbency and drape. Both types are widely used in industries like hygiene products, wipes, and medical supplies.</p></p>
