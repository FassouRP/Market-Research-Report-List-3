<p><strong>The Resin Sand Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Resin Coated Sand</li><li>Resin Bonded Sand</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Resin sand market mainly consists of two types: resin coated sand and resin bonded sand. Resin coated sand is a type of foundry sand with a high-quality resin added to a silica sand base. This process enhances the surface characteristics of the sand grains, resulting in better mold quality. On the other hand, resin bonded sand is a mixture of resin and sand that is used for making molds and cores in foundry applications. Both types offer improved casting quality and are widely used in the manufacturing industry.</p></p>
