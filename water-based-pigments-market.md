<p><p>Water based pigments are classified into two main types: organic water based pigments and inorganic water based pigments. Organic water based pigments are derived from natural sources such as plants and animals, and are known for their vibrant colors and eco-friendly properties. Inorganic water based pigments, on the other hand, are synthetically produced and offer a wider range of colors and superior lightfastness. Both types of pigments are widely used in various industries such as paints, coatings, and textiles.</p></p>
