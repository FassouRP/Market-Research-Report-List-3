<p><p>Liquid Silicone Rubber (LSR) for industrial applications is commonly categorized into two main market types: one-component and two-component liquid rubber. One-component LSR is ready to use and cures at room temperature, making it ideal for quick and simple applications. On the other hand, two-component LSR requires mixing of a base and a catalyst before use, allowing for more precise control over curing times and properties. Both types offer excellent durability, resistance to extreme temperatures, and flexibility, making them popular choices in various industrial sectors.</p></p>
