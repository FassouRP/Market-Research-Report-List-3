<p><strong>The Electric Power Steering (EPS) Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Torque Only Sensor(TOS)</li><li>Torque Angle Sensor(TAS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electric Power Steering (EPS) Sensor Market consists of two main types: Torque Only Sensor (TOS) and Torque Angle Sensor (TAS). TOS measures only the steering torque applied by the driver, providing feedback to the EPS system to assist with steering. TAS, on the other hand, measures both the steering torque and the angle of the steering wheel, offering more precise control and feedback to the EPS system. Both types of sensors play a crucial role in enhancing the performance and efficiency of EPS systems in modern vehicles.</p></p>
