<p><p>Switching PTC thermistors are used in two main market types: Self Heating Mode and Sensor Mode. In Self Heating Mode, the thermistor is used to trigger a switch by generating heat when a specified current passes through it. In Sensor Mode, the thermistor acts as a temperature sensor by changing its resistance based on temperature fluctuations. Both modes play a crucial role in various applications, from controlling electronic devices to monitoring environmental conditions.</p></p>
