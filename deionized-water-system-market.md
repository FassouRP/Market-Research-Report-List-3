<p><strong>The Deionized Water System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Two-bed Deionized Water System</li><li>Mixed-bed Deionized Water System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Deionized water systems are commonly classified into two types: two-bed deionized water systems and mixed-bed deionized water systems. Two-bed deionized water systems consist of separate tanks for cation and anion resin beds, which effectively remove ions from the water. On the other hand, mixed-bed deionized water systems have a single tank with mixed cation and anion resins for more thorough ion removal. Both systems are essential in various industries for producing high-quality deionized water for specific applications.</p></p>
