<p><p>PVDF for Li-Ion Batteries is commonly produced through two types of polymerization processes: Emulsion Polymerization and Suspension Polymerization. Emulsion Polymerization involves dispersing PVDF monomers in an aqueous medium with surfactants to form droplets that polymerize into a latex. Suspension Polymerization, on the other hand, involves suspending PVDF monomers in a non-aqueous medium with stabilizers to form spherical particles that polymerize. Both methods result in PVDF with different properties, making them suitable for various applications in the Li-Ion Batteries market.</p></p>
