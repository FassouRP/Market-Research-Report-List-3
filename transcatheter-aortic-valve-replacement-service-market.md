<p><strong>The Transcatheter Aortic Valve Replacement Service Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Aortic Stenosis Replacement</li><li>Aortic Regurgitation Replacement</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Transcatheter Aortic Valve Replacement (TAVR) service market includes two main types of procedures: Aortic Stenosis Replacement and Aortic Regurgitation Replacement. Aortic Stenosis Replacement refers to the replacement of the narrowed aortic valve due to aortic stenosis, a condition where the valve fails to open fully, leading to restricted blood flow from the heart. Aortic Regurgitation Replacement, on the other hand, involves replacing aortic valves that fail to close properly, leading to a leakage of blood back into the heart. Both procedures are performed using minimally invasive transcatheter techniques instead of open-heart surgery.</p></p>
