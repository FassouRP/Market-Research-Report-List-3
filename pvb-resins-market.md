<p><p>PVB resins are used in various industries such as automotive, construction, and electronics. The market for PVB resins is divided into two main types based on viscosity: high viscosity and low viscosity. High viscosity PVB resins are thicker and offer enhanced strength and durability, making them suitable for applications requiring greater impact resistance. On the other hand, low viscosity PVB resins are more fluid and provide better clarity, making them ideal for applications like laminated safety glass in automotive windshields.</p></p>
