<p><p>Piezoelectric surgery devices are a type of surgical instruments that use ultrasonic vibrations to cut and remove hard tissues in various surgical procedures. Firstly, the increasing prevalence of dental and orthopedic diseases is driving the demand for precise and minimally invasive surgical techniques, which can be facilitated by piezoelectric surgery devices. Secondly, the rising geriatric population, who are more prone to dental and musculoskeletal disorders, is also boosting the demand for these devices. Additionally, advancements in piezoelectric technology, such as the development of piezoceramic materials and improved instrumentation, are further contributing to market growth.</p><p>Moreover, the market is witnessing several trends that are shaping its growth trajectory. One such trend is the development of multifunctional piezoelectric surgery devices that can perform multiple surgical procedures, thereby reducing the need for multiple instruments. Another trend is the increasing adoption of these devices in ambulatory surgical centers and dental clinics, owing to their efficiency and cost-effectiveness. Furthermore, manufacturers are focusing on improving ergonomics and user-friendly features of these devices to enhance surgical precision and reduce user fatigue.</p><p>According to the forecast, the piezoelectric surgery devices market is expected to grow at a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 9.7% during the forecast period.
