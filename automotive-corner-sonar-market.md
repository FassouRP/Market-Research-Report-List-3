<p><p>Automotive corner sonar systems can be categorized into two main types: passive sonar and active sonar. Passive sonar systems detect sound waves generated by other vehicles or objects, providing information on their location and movement without emitting any signals. Active sonar systems, on the other hand, emit sound waves and analyze the echoes to gather information about surrounding obstacles. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing driver safety by providing real-time data on the vehicle's surroundings, helping to prevent accidents.</p></p>
