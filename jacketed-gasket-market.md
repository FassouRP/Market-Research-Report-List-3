<p><p>Jacketed gaskets are classified into three main types based on the material used in their construction: semi-metallic, non-metallic, and metallic. Semi-metallic gaskets combine the sealing capabilities of both metal and non-metal materials, offering a balance of strength and flexibility. Non-metallic gaskets are made from materials such as rubber, graphite, or PTFE, providing excellent chemical resistance and insulation properties. Metallic gaskets are constructed entirely from metal, offering high temperature and pressure resistance. Each type of jacketed gasket is designed to meet specific industry requirements and applications.</p></p>
