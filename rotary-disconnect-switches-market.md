<p><ul><li>Fused Rotary Disconnect Switches</li><li>Non-Fused Rotary Disconnect Switches</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rotary disconnect switches are categorized into two main types: fused and non-fused. Fused rotary disconnect switches include built-in fuses for overcurrent protection, making them suitable for applications where safety is a top priority. On the other hand, non-fused rotary disconnect switches do not have built-in fuses, offering a simpler design and lower cost. Both types of switches provide a means to safely disconnect power to equipment for maintenance or emergency purposes, enhancing overall safety in industrial settings.</p></p>
