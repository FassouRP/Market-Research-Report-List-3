<p><p>Medium Chain Triglycerides Oil (MCT Oil) is categorized into two main types based on the length of the carbon chain - C6-C8 and C10-C12. C6-C8 MCT oil is made up of caproic acid and caprylic acid, which are quickly absorbed by the body and provide fast energy. On the other hand, C10-C12 MCT oil contains capric acid and lauric acid, which are slower to digest but offer longer sustained energy. Both types of MCT oils are popular in the health and wellness industry for their various benefits.</p></p>
