<p><p>Multi-head weighing machines are commonly used in the packaging industry to accurately weigh and dispense products. There are two main types of multi-head weighing machines: linear and rotary. Linear machines have a linear arrangement of weighing heads, making them ideal for larger products or irregular shapes. Rotary machines have a circular arrangement of weighing heads, making them better suited for smaller products or higher speeds. Both types offer high-speed weighing capabilities and can improve efficiency in packaging operations.</p></p>
