<p><p>Wireless Ambulatory Telemetry Monitors are medical devices used for remote monitoring of patients' vital signs and physiological parameters. They enable real-time monitoring of patients' heart rate, blood pressure, oxygen saturation, and other vital signs, allowing healthcare professionals to track the patient's condition continuously.</p><p>The Wireless Ambulatory Telemetry Monitors Market is experiencing significant growth due to several factors. The market is driven by the increasing prevalence of chronic diseases, such as cardiovascular disorders and respiratory diseases, which require continuous monitoring of patients.
