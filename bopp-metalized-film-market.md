<p><p>BOPP Metalized Film market comprises two main types: General CPP Metallized Film and Low Temperature Heat Seal CPP Metallized Film. General CPP Metallized Film is commonly used for packaging applications due to its high barrier properties and good machinability. On the other hand, Low Temperature Heat Seal CPP Metallized Film is designed for applications requiring a lower heat sealing temperature, making it suitable for heat-sensitive products. Both types of films offer benefits such as improved shelf life and protection against moisture and oxygen.</p></p>
