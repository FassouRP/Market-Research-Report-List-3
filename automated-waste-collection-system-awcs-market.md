<p><p>Automated Waste Collection Systems (AWCS) are innovative solutions that efficiently collect and transport waste within buildings. The market for AWCS can be classified into two types: the Gravity System and the Full Vacuum System. The Gravity System uses the force of gravity to move waste through underground pipes, while the Full Vacuum System uses suction to transport waste in a closed network of pipes. Both systems offer convenient waste disposal, eliminate manual handling, improve hygiene, and reduce environmental impact, providing sustainable waste management solutions for various buildings and communities.</p></p>
