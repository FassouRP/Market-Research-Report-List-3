<p><p>The solvent-free resins market can be categorized into three main types: single component, two components, and three components. Single component resins are pre-mixed and ready to use, simplifying the application process. Two component resins consist of a resin and hardener that are mixed together before application, offering greater control over curing time and properties. Three component resins include an additional additive, typically a filler or modifier, for enhanced performance and customization. Each type of solvent-free resin offers unique advantages for various applications.</p></p>
