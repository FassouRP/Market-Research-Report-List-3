<p><strong>The Backlighting Components Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Edge Lighting</li><li>Bottom Lighting</li><li>Hollow Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In the backlighting components market, there are three main types: Edge Lighting, Bottom Lighting, and Hollow Type. Edge Lighting involves placing LED lights along the edges of a display panel to illuminate the entire surface evenly. Bottom Lighting consists of placing LED lights at the bottom of a display to provide uniform backlighting. Hollow Type involves placing LED lights around the edges of a hollow structure to create a glowing effect. Each type offers unique advantages and is used in various applications depending on the desired lighting effect.</p></p>
