<p><strong>The Integrated Touchscreen Module Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>On-Cell</li><li>In-Cell</li></ul></p>
<p><p>On-Cell and In-Cell are two types of integrated touchscreen modules used in electronic devices. On-Cell technology involves placing the touch sensor on the top layer of the display screen, while In-Cell technology integrates the touch sensor directly into the LCD panel. On-Cell offers improved touch sensitivity and lower production costs, while In-Cell provides a thinner and lighter screen design. Both technologies aim to enhance the user experience by offering more responsive and aesthetically pleasing touchscreen displays.</p></p>
