<p><p>Laminate tube packaging market types include ABL (aluminum barrier laminate) and PBL (plastic barrier laminate). ABL tubes consist of multiple layers of aluminum and plastic, providing excellent barrier properties for preserving products. PBL tubes are made with layers of plastic materials, offering a flexible and cost-effective packaging solution. Both types of laminate tubes are widely used in the pharmaceutical, cosmetics, and food industries for various products such as creams, ointments, toothpaste, and more. These packaging options ensure product safety, protection, and shelf-life extension.</p></p>
