<p><p>Polyethylene market includes High-Density Polyethylene (HDPE), Low-Density Polyethylene (LDPE), Linear Low-Density Polyethylene (LLDPE), and other types. HDPE is known for its high strength and durability, commonly used in containers and pipes. LDPE is flexible and widely used in packaging. LLDPE is a blend of HDPE and LDPE, offering flexibility and impact resistance, often used in packaging films. Other types of polyethylene include UHMWPE, used in medical devices, and VLDPE, used in industrial applications. Each type serves different purposes based on their unique properties.</p></p>
