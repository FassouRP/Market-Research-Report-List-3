<p><p>Gas delivery systems market is segmented into three types - Signal Station Systems, Semi-automatic Switchover Systems, and Fully Automatic Programmable Switchover Systems. Signal Station Systems involve manual monitoring and adjustment of gas delivery, while Semi-automatic Switchover Systems require minimal human intervention for switching between gas sources. Fully Automatic Programmable Switchover Systems are advanced systems that can automatically detect gas source levels and switch between them based on pre-programmed settings. Each type caters to different needs and preferences in the gas delivery industry.</p></p>
