<p><ul><li>C Type</li><li>E Type</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Iron-based nanocrystalline cores are commonly classified into three main types in the market: C Type, E Type, and Others. C Type cores are known for their high permeability and low coercivity, making them suitable for high frequency applications. E Type cores, on the other hand, are favored for their high saturation flux density and low core loss, making them ideal for power applications. The Others category includes cores with special shapes or unique properties that do not fall into the C or E Type classifications.</p></p>
