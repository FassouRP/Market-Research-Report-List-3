<p><p>The oxygen supply system market comprises various types of equipment that deliver oxygen to patients in need. Oxygen masks cover the nose and mouth, providing a high amount of oxygen for patients with severe respiratory issues. Nasal cannulas are small tubes that deliver oxygen directly into the nostrils, suitable for patients with mild respiratory issues. Non-rebreather masks offer a higher concentration of oxygen and have a reservoir bag for breathing assistance. Bag valve masks are handheld devices used by healthcare professionals to manually provide oxygen to patients. Other types include face tents, tracheostomy masks, and oxygen hoods with specific applications.</p></p>
