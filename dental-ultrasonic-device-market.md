<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Dental Ultrasonic Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Magnetostrictive</li><li>Piezoelectric</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental ultrasonic device market comprises two types: magnetostrictive and piezoelectric devices. Magnetostrictive devices utilize the vibration of a metal stack to generate high-frequency sound waves and are typically more powerful, making them suitable for heavy-duty procedures. Piezoelectric devices, on the other hand, use crystals that expand and contract when an electric current is applied, generating ultrasonic vibrations. They are generally more gentle and precise, making them ideal for delicate treatments. Both device types offer varying benefits and cater to different dental procedures, aligning with the specific needs of practitioners and patients.</p></p>
