<p><p>Electrically-actuated valves are used for controlling the flow of fluids in various industries. Nozzle flapper valves offer precise control by using a nozzle to direct flow. Jet action valves use a jet of fluid to regulate the opening and closing of the valve. Dynamic valves have moving parts that change position based on electrical signals to control flow. Each type of electrically-actuated valve has unique characteristics and advantages, making them suitable for different applications in industries such as manufacturing, oil and gas, and water treatment.</p></p>
