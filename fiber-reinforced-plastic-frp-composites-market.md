The main types of fibers used in FRP composites are glass fibers, carbon fibers, and aramid fibers. Glass fibers are cost-effective and widely used in various applications. Carbon fibers offer high strength and stiffness, making them suitable for demanding applications. Aramid fibers provide excellent impact resistance and are commonly used in ballistic protection. Other types of fibers, such as natural fibers, are also emerging in the FRP composites market.</p></p>
