<p><p>Motor vehicle sensors market is divided into various types based on their functions and applications. Pressure sensors are used to monitor air pressure in tires and engine intake systems. Temperature sensors help regulate engine temperature to prevent overheating. Speed sensors measure vehicle speed and send signals to control systems. Position sensors detect the position of moving parts such as throttle and gearbox. O2 sensors monitor oxygen levels in exhaust gases to ensure efficient fuel combustion. Other sensors may include level sensors, proximity sensors, and image sensors for advanced driver assistance systems.</p></p>
