The market for these resins includes polyester, epoxy, vinyl ester, phenolic, polyurethane, and high temperature resins. Polyester resins are commonly used in construction, while epoxy resins are popular for their strong adhesive properties. Vinyl ester resins are known for their corrosion resistance, while phenolic resins are favored for their heat resistance. Polyurethane resins offer flexibility and durability, and high temperature resins are used in applications requiring extreme heat resistance.</p></p>
