<p><p>Printing auxiliaries market types can be categorized into textile chemical thickener, adhesive agent, crosslinking agent, emulsifier agent, fixing agent, and other miscellaneous additives. Textile chemical thickener is used to increase the viscosity of printing pastes for better print definition. Adhesive agents help in binding the pigment to the fabric surface, while crosslinking agents improve the washfastness of the prints. Emulsifier agents aid in dispersing the pigment uniformly in the printing paste, and fixing agents help in retaining the color on the fabric. Other miscellaneous additives serve various functions in the printing process.</p></p>
