<p><strong>The Investment Casting Parts Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sodium Silicate Process</li><li>Tetraethyl Orthosilicate/ Silica Sol Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Investment casting parts market types refer to different methods used in the manufacturing process. The sodium silicate process involves creating a mold using a mixture of sodium silicate and refractory aggregate, which is then hardened with carbon dioxide. On the other hand, the tetraethyl orthosilicate/silica sol process involves using a silica sol binder to create the mold, which is then hardened by the addition of tetraethyl orthosilicate. Both processes are commonly used in the production of precision parts for various industries.</p></p>
