<p><p>Viscosity Modifying Admixtures (VMA) are substances added to concrete to enhance its workability and resistance to segregation. There are two main types of VMA markets: Liquid Viscosity Modifying Admixtures and Dry Powder Viscosity Modifying Admixtures. Liquid VMAs are typically added directly to the concrete mix in liquid form, while Dry Powder VMAs are added in a powdered form. Both types of VMAs serve to improve the rheological properties of concrete, making it easier to handle and ensuring uniform distribution of aggregates.</p></p>
