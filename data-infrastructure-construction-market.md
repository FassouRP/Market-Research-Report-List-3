<p><p>Data infrastructure construction market types refer to the different categories of cellular network technologies used to establish data connectivity. Femtocell is a small, low-power cell designed for indoor coverage in home or office environments. Pico cell provides coverage to a slightly larger area, typically within buildings or small outdoor areas. Micro cell covers a larger area, such as a neighborhood or campus. Macro cell is the largest and most powerful, providing coverage to a wide geographical region, such as a city. These types vary in size, power, and coverage area to cater to different data connectivity requirements.</p></p>
