<p><p>High Electron Mobility Transistors (HEMTs) are advanced semiconductor devices used for high-frequency applications. GaN HEMTs offer high power efficiency and high breakdown voltage, making them ideal for power electronics. GaN/SiC HEMTs combine the benefits of both materials for even higher performance. GaAs HEMTs are widely used in high-frequency and high-speed applications due to their high electron mobility.</p></p>
