<p><p>Elemental Boron is available in two main market types: Amorphous Boron and Crystalline Boron. Amorphous Boron is a non-crystalline form of the element with a disordered atomic structure, making it versatile and easily molded into various shapes for applications such as coatings and composites. Crystalline Boron, on the other hand, has a highly ordered atomic structure and greater hardness, making it ideal for high-tech applications like semiconductors and aerospace materials. Both types cater to different industries and offer unique properties for specific uses.</p></p>
