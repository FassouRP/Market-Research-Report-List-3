<p><p>Residential exhaust ventilation systems are designed to improve indoor air quality by removing pollutants and excess moisture. There are three main types of residential exhaust ventilation systems: ceiling type, wall-mounted type, and others. Ceiling type ventilation systems are installed in the ceiling and work by extracting air from the room and expelling it outside. Wall-mounted systems are installed on walls and operate similarly to ceiling types. Other ventilation systems may include inline duct fans or combination exhaust fans with heat recovery capabilities.</p></p>
