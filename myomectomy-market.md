<p><p>Myomectomy is a surgical procedure performed to remove uterine fibroids, which are noncancerous growths in the uterus. There are different types of myomectomy procedures available in the market. Abdominal myomectomy involves making an incision in the abdomen to remove fibroids. Hysteroscopic myomectomy is a less invasive technique that uses a hysteroscope to remove fibroids through the vagina. Laparoscopic myomectomy utilizes small incisions and a laparoscope for fibroid removal. These different types offer alternatives to women depending on the size, location, and number of fibroids, allowing for individualized treatment options.</p></p>
