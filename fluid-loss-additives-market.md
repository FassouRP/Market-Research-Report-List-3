<p><p>Fluid loss additives play a crucial role in managing fluid loss during drilling operations in the oil and gas industry. There are three main types of fluid loss additives: synthetically modified natural additives (such as PAC and CMC), synthetic additives (like acrylic sulphonated polymers), and natural additives (including starch, lignin, and bentonite). These additives help control fluid loss by forming a filter cake on the walls of the wellbore, preventing excessive fluid loss and maintaining stability in the drilling fluid. Each type of additive offers unique benefits and applications in different drilling scenarios.</p></p>
