<p><p>The passenger vehicle replacement tire market consists of two main types: radial tires and bias tires. Radial tires are constructed with layers of fabric cords positioned at 90 degrees to the direction of travel, providing better traction, fuel efficiency, and durability. Bias tires, on the other hand, have overlapping plies angled at 30-40 degrees, offering a smoother ride but less fuel efficiency and durability compared to radial tires. Both types cater to different consumer preferences and driving conditions in the passenger vehicle replacement tire market.</p></p>
