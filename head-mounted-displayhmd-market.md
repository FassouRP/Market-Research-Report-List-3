<p><p>Head Mounted Displays (HMDs) are categorized into Slide-on HMD, Discrete HMD, and Integrated HMD markets. Slide-on HMDs are designed to be attached to existing headwear, providing a more flexible and customizable option. Discrete HMDs are standalone devices that can be worn independently, offering convenience and portability. Integrated HMDs are incorporated into a piece of equipment or system, often used in specialized industries such as military, medical, and automotive. Each type caters to different needs and preferences in the growing HMD market.</p></p>
