<p><p>Semiconductor manufacturing waste gas treatment market involves various types of treatment methods for managing waste gases generated during the manufacturing process. Direct incineration refers to burning the waste gases at high temperatures to convert them into harmless substances. Activated carbon adsorption involves trapping and removing the harmful gases using activated carbon material. Biological oxidation utilizes microorganisms to break down the waste gases into less harmful components. Other treatment methods may include chemical scrubbing, wet scrubbing, and catalytic oxidation. These methods play a crucial role in reducing air pollution and ensuring a safer environment in semiconductor manufacturing facilities.</p></p>
