<p><p>The Low Pressure Vacuum Carburizing Furnaces Market is categorized into three types: Single Chamber, Double Chamber, and Multi Chamber. Single Chamber furnaces have one chamber where the carburizing process takes place, making them suitable for small-scale applications. Double Chamber furnaces consist of two chambers, allowing for simultaneous loading and unloading of workpieces, increasing productivity. Multi Chamber furnaces have multiple chambers with independent controls, enabling different heat treatment processes to be carried out simultaneously. Each type caters to specific needs and requirements of different industries.</p></p>
