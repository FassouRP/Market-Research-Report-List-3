<p><p>Mechanical suspension seats utilize a system of springs and dampers to absorb shock and provide support to the user. These seats are cost-effective and suitable for applications where minimal vibration control is needed. On the other hand, air suspension seats use compressed air to adjust the seat height and cushion firmness, providing a more customizable and comfortable seating experience. These seats are ideal for heavy-duty equipment where operators spend long hours seated, as they offer superior shock absorption and ergonomic support.</p></p>
