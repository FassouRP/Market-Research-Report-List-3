<p><p>Automotive seatbelt pre-tensioners are designed to tighten the seatbelt in the event of a collision to reduce the forward movement of the occupant. There are three main types of pre-tensioners in the market: buckle pretensioner, retractor pretensioner, and others. Buckle pretensioners tighten the seatbelt at the buckle end, while retractor pretensioners tighten the belt at the retractor end. Other types of pre-tensioners may include dual-stage or pyrotechnic pre-tensioners that use different mechanisms to achieve the same goal of minimizing occupant movement during a crash.</p></p>
