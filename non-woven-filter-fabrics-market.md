<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Non-woven Filter Fabrics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Needle Punched Nonwoven</li><li>Spunbond Nonwoven</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Needle punched nonwoven filter fabrics are made by mechanically punching needle-like barbs into the fibers to entangle them, creating a dense and durable material suitable for high-efficiency filtration. Spunbond nonwoven filter fabrics, on the other hand, are made by extruding continuous filaments of polypropylene or polyester, then bonding them together with heat and pressure to form a strong and uniform fabric. Both types of nonwoven filter fabrics are widely used in various industries for applications requiring high filtration efficiency and durability.</p></p>
