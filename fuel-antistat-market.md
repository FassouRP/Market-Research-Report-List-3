<p><p>Fuel Antistat market is divided into two main types: Metal Antistatic Agents and Non-Metallic Antistatic Agents. Metal antistatic agents are typically composed of metal ions which function to neutralize the static charge in fuel. On the other hand, non-metallic antistatic agents utilize organic compounds or additives to achieve the same effect. Both types of antistatic agents work to prevent the build-up of static electricity in fuel, which can be dangerous in certain environments.</p></p>
