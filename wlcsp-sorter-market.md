Wafer Sorters are used to inspect and sort wafers before they are diced into individual chips. Die Sorters handle the sorting and packaging of individual dies after dicing. Test Sorters are used to perform electrical testing on the individual chips to ensure they meet quality standards. Each type of sorter plays a crucial role in the overall production process of WLCSP devices.</p></p>
