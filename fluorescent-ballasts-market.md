<p><p>Fluorescent Ballasts are an essential component in the functioning of fluorescent lights, regulating the current that flows through the lamp. There are two main types of Fluorescent Ballasts - Magnetic Fluorescent Ballasts and Electronic Fluorescent Ballasts. Magnetic Ballasts are traditional, using inductive coils to control the flow of electricity, while Electronic Ballasts are newer and more efficient, using electronic circuitry to regulate the current. Both types have their own advantages and are used in various lighting applications.</p></p>
