<p><p>Paper Phenolic Copper Clad Laminate market offers two main types - Single-sided Copper Clad and Double-sided Copper Clad. Single-sided copper clad laminate is a type where copper is only plated on one side of the paper phenolic board, while Double-sided copper clad laminate has copper plated on both sides. Single-sided copper clad is commonly used for simpler circuits, while double-sided copper clad is preferred for more complex circuit designs requiring connections on both sides of the board.</p></p>
