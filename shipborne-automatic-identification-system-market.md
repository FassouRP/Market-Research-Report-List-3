<p><ul><li>Class A</li><li>Class B</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Shipborne Automatic Identification System (AIS) market is segmented into two major types - Class A and Class B. Class A systems are mandatory for vessels over 300 gross tons and are required to transmit and receive AIS messages at regular intervals, providing detailed vessel information. On the other hand, Class B systems are typically used by smaller vessels and are not mandatory. They have a lower transmission power and provide less information compared to Class A systems. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing maritime safety and navigation.</p></p>
