<p><p>Polyurethane foam insulation materials market includes four main types: Flexible Polyurethane Foam (FDF) which is used in furniture, bedding, and automotive applications due to its soft and flexible nature. Semi-Flexible Polyurethane Foam is a mix between FDF and RPF, offering some rigidity while still being somewhat flexible. Rigid Polyurethane Foam (RPF) is used in construction for its high insulation properties and structural support. Spray Polyurethane Foam (SPF) is applied as a liquid, expanding to fill gaps and cavities, commonly used in insulation and sealing applications.</p></p>
