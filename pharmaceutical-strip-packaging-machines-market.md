<p><p>Pharmaceutical strip packaging machines are categorized into three main types based on their functions. Primary packaging machines are used for directly packaging the medication into strips. Secondary packaging machines are utilized for grouping these individual strips into larger packaging units such as boxes or cartons. Labeling and serialization machines are responsible for adding important information and unique serial numbers to the packaging for tracking and compliance purposes. Each of these machines plays a crucial role in the pharmaceutical packaging process.</p></p>
