<p><p>Automatic balanced control valves are commonly available in two main types: thread type and flange type. Thread type valves are designed to be easily screwed onto the piping system, making them more convenient for installation and maintenance. On the other hand, flange type valves are connected to the piping system using bolts and gaskets, providing a more secure and leak-proof connection. Both types cater to different installation preferences and requirements in the market for automatic balanced control valves.</p></p>
