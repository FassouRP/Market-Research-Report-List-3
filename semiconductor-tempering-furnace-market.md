<p><ul><li>Vertical Furnace</li><li>Horizontal Furnace</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Semiconductor tempering furnaces are classified into three types based on their design and orientation: vertical furnace, horizontal furnace, and others. Vertical furnaces have a vertical chamber where the wafers are stacked vertically, allowing for efficient heating and cooling processes. Horizontal furnaces have a horizontal orientation, with wafers placed on a rotating quartz boat for uniform heating. The "Others" category includes specialized designs that do not fit into the vertical or horizontal classifications, offering unique features and capabilities for specific semiconductor processing needs.</p></p>
