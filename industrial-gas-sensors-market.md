<p><p>Industrial gas sensors market is divided into four main types: electrochemical, semiconductor, infrared, and photoionization detector (PID). Electrochemical sensors use a chemical reaction to detect gases, semiconductor sensors use a semiconductor material to detect gas concentrations, infrared sensors measure gas levels by analyzing wavelengths of light absorbed, and PID sensors use ultraviolet light to ionize gas molecules for detection. Each type of sensor has its own strengths and weaknesses, making them suitable for different industrial applications.</p></p>
