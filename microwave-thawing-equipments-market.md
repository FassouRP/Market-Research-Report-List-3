<p><p>Microwave thawing equipment market encompasses various types such as thawing chambers, roller thawing machines, and thawing continuous lines. Thawing chambers are designed for small-scale thawing of food products, while roller thawing machines are suitable for continuous and uniform thawing of larger quantities. Thawing continuous lines are industrial-scale equipment used for rapidly and efficiently thawing large volumes of food products. Each type caters to different needs and capacities within the food processing industry, providing options for businesses of various sizes.</p></p>
