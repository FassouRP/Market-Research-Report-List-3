<p><ul><li>Feeder Protection Relay</li><li>Differential Protection Relay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Medium Voltage Motor Protectors are utilized to prevent faults or electrical issues in motors. Feeder Protection Relays specifically monitor the electrical supply to the motor, ensuring stable voltage and current levels. On the other hand, Differential Protection Relays detect any imbalances between incoming and outgoing currents, indicating a fault within the motor system. Both types of protectors are essential for maintaining the efficiency and safety of medium voltage motors, providing real-time monitoring and quick response to potential issues.</p></p>
