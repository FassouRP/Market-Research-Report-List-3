<p><p>The plastic cable cleat market can be divided into three main types based on the design and functionality of the cleats. Single type cleats are designed to secure individual cables, while multicore type cleats are specifically designed to secure multiple cables together. Trefoil type cleats are used for securing three-phase cables in a triangular configuration to prevent damage and maintain proper cable alignment. Each type of cleat serves a specific purpose in cable management and plays a crucial role in maintaining the safety and reliability of electrical installations.</p></p>
