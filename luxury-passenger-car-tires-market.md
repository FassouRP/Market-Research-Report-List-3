<p><ul><li>Bias Tire</li><li>Radial Tire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Luxury passenger car tires come in two main types: Bias tires and Radial tires. Bias tires are constructed with multiple rubber-coated plies laid diagonally across each other, providing good stability and durability. On the other hand, Radial tires have plies that run radially from bead to bead, allowing for better grip, handling, and fuel efficiency. Both types have their own advantages and are suited for different driving conditions and preferences in the luxury passenger car market.</p></p>
