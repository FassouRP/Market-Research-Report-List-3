<p><p>Fatty alcohols can be categorized based on the number of carbon atoms in their chain. C6-C10 fatty alcohols are commonly used in personal care products for their emollient properties. C11-C14 fatty alcohols are widely used in the production of surfactants for various industries. C15-C22 fatty alcohols are used in the manufacturing of detergents and lubricants due to their cleaning and lubricating properties. C23 fatty alcohols are less common and are primarily used in niche industrial applications.</p></p>
