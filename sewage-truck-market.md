<p><p>Sewage truck market is divided into three main types: front loaders, rear loaders, and side loaders. Front loaders are equipped with a hydraulic arm that lifts and empties bins into the truck's storage compartment at the front. Rear loaders have a large opening at the back for manual loading of waste. Side loaders are designed to collect waste from the side of the road using a mechanical arm. Each type caters to different waste collection requirements and preferences of municipalities and waste management companies.</p></p>
