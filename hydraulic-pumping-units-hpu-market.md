The two main types of HPUs are Beam Pumping Units and Beamless Pumping Units. Beam Pumping Units utilize a beam and walking beam configuration to pump oil, while Beamless Pumping Units are more compact and do not have a traditional beam structure. Both types are crucial in the efficient and reliable operation of oil and gas wells.</p></p>
