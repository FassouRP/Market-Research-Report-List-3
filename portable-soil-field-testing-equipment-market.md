<p><p>Portable Soil Field Testing Equipment includes various types of equipment used for on-site testing of soil properties. Soil Sampling Tests involve collecting soil samples for analysis. Triaxial Tests are conducted to measure the strength and stress-strain behavior of soil. CBR Tests determine the bearing capacity of soil. Density & Consolidation Tests assess the compactness and settlement characteristics of soil. Water Permeability Tests measure the rate at which water can pass through soil. These tests help in evaluating the quality and characteristics of soil for construction and agricultural purposes.</p></p>
