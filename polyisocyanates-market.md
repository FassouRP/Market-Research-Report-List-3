<p><p>Polyisocyanates can be classified into three main types: aromatic polyisocyanates, aliphatic polyisocyanates, and other polyisocyanates. Aromatic polyisocyanates are commonly used in coatings for their high reactivity and excellent chemical resistance. Aliphatic polyisocyanates, on the other hand, offer superior UV resistance and color stability, making them ideal for outdoor applications. Other polyisocyanates encompass a range of specialty products tailored for specific performance requirements, such as flexibility, adhesion, or thermal resistance. Each type of polyisocyanate serves different purposes in various industries.</p></p>
