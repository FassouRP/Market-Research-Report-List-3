<p><p>Building Maintenance Units (BMU) are equipment used for cleaning and maintaining the exterior of high-rise buildings. There are two main types of BMU control systems: Manual Control and Electric Control. In Manual Control systems, operators manually control the movement of the unit using hand cranks or levers. On the other hand, Electric Control systems utilize motors and automated controls for movement. Both types cater to different market needs and preferences, with Electric Control BMUs typically being more efficient and user-friendly compared to Manual Control BMUs.</p></p>
