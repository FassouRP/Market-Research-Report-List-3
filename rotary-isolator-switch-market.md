There are two main types of rotary isolator switches: single pole and multi-pole. Single pole isolator switches are designed to disconnect one circuit, while multi-pole isolator switches have the capability to disconnect multiple circuits simultaneously. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring safety and preventing electrical hazards in various applications such as industrial, commercial, and residential settings.</p></p>
