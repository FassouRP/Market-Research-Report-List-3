<p><p>The residential lightning rod market consists of various types such as air-termination lightning protection system, meshed conductors lightning protection system, catenary wire lightning conductor, streamer emission lightning conductor, and others. Air-termination systems provide protection by attracting lightning strikes, while meshed conductors disperse the electrical charge across the structure. Catenary wire conductors create a path for lightning to follow to the ground, and streamer emission conductors emit ions to prevent lightning strikes. Other systems offer alternative methods of lightning protection for residential buildings.</p></p>
