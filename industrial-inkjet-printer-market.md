<p><p>The industrial inkjet printer market can be categorized into three types: small character inkjet printers, large character inkjet printers, and high-resolution inkjet printers. Small character inkjet printers are primarily used for printing on small surfaces, such as packaging materials, with high speed and accuracy. Large character inkjet printers are suitable for printing on larger surfaces, like corrugated boxes, providing bold and clear characters. High-resolution inkjet printers are designed for precision printing on products with fine details, such as labels or circuit boards, ensuring sharp and high-quality prints.</p></p>
