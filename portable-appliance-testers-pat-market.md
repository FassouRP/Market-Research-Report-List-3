<p><p>Portable Appliance Testers (PAT) are used to test the safety of electrical appliances in various markets. Fail/Pass PAT Testing determines if an appliance meets safety standards or not. Earth bond Testing checks if an appliance has adequate grounding. Insulation Testing ensures the electrical insulation is sufficient. Battery Powered Testing evaluates the performance of battery-operated devices. Leakage/load Testing assesses the leakage currents and load capacities of appliances. These tests are essential to ensure the safety and functionality of electrical appliances across different industries.</p></p>
