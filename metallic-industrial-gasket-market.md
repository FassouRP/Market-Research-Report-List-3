Ring Joint gaskets are designed for high-pressure applications, with a metal ring providing excellent sealing. Corrugated gaskets have ridges for increased flexibility and durability. Other types may include Kammprofile and Double Jacketed gaskets, offering unique sealing properties for specific industrial applications. Each type caters to different sealing requirements in industrial settings.</p></p>
