<p><p>Polyethylene Naphthalate (PEN) is a type of polyester resin used in various industries. There are two main processes for producing PEN: Ester process and Acid process. The Ester process involves the reaction of naphthalene dicarboxylic acid with ethylene glycol, while the Acid process involves the reaction of dimethyl naphthalate with ethylene glycol. Both processes result in the production of high-quality PEN resin, which is used in applications such as packaging, electronics, and automotive components.</p></p>
