<p><p>Isocyanates market is categorized into three main types: ADI (Aliphatic Diisocyanates), MDI (Methylene Diphenyl Diisocyanate), and TDI (Toluene Diisocyanate). ADI is used in specialty coatings, adhesives, and sealants due to its high resistance properties. MDI is used in rigid foam insulation, coatings, paints, and adhesives. TDI is primarily used in flexible foam insulation, automotive seating, and cushioning materials. Each type serves different industries and applications based on their characteristics and performance.</p></p>
