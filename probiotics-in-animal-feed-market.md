<p><strong>The Probiotics in Animal Feed Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bacteria Source</li><li>Yeast Source</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Probiotics in animal feed can be categorized based on the source of the microorganisms used, such as bacteria source probiotics or yeast source probiotics. Bacteria source probiotics mainly consist of strains like Lactobacillus and Bifidobacterium, which are commonly found in the gastrointestinal tract of animals. Yeast source probiotics, on the other hand, typically include Saccharomyces cerevisiae and other beneficial yeast strains. Both types of probiotics are used to improve gut health, digestion, and overall animal performance in the livestock industry.</p></p>
