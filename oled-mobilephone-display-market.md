<p><strong>The OLED Mobilephone Display Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PMOLED</li><li>AMOLED</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PMOLED stands for Passive Matrix Organic Light Emitting Diode, and AMOLED stands for Active Matrix Organic Light Emitting Diode. PMOLED displays are simpler and less expensive, with each row and column being controlled independently, limiting their size and resolution. On the other hand, AMOLED displays are more advanced and offer better color reproduction and energy efficiency due to each pixel being controlled individually. AMOLED displays are commonly used in high-end smartphones for their superior performance.</p></p>
