<p><p>Conventional Fluid Bed Dryers can be classified into two main types: Batch Operation and Continuous Operation. Batch Operation involves inputting a specific quantity of material into the dryer and processing it in batches, while Continuous Operation allows for a continuous flow of material through the dryer. Batch Operation is typically used for smaller quantities and varied processing times, while Continuous Operation is ideal for large-scale production with a constant flow of material. Both types offer their own unique advantages and are suitable for different production needs.</p></p>
