<p><p>Inline Automated Optical Inspection (AOI) Equipment refers to equipment used in manufacturing processes to inspect printed circuit boards for defects or abnormalities. There are two main types of AOI equipment: 2D AOI Equipment and 3D AOI Equipment. </p><p>2D AOI Equipment uses cameras to capture 2D images of PCBs and compare them to a reference image for defects. </p><p>On the other hand, 3D AOI Equipment utilizes advanced sensors and algorithms to create a detailed 3D image of the PCB surface, allowing for more accurate defect detection and measurement. Both types of equipment play a crucial role in ensuring the quality and reliability of electronic products.</p></p>
