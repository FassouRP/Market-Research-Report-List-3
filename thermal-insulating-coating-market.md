<p><p>Thermal insulating coatings are categorized based on their temperature resistance capabilities. Coatings with temperature resistance less than 200℃ are typically used for low-temperature applications such as household appliances or light industrial equipment. Those with temperature resistance between 200-600℃ are suitable for medium-temperature environments like automotive engines or pipelines. Coatings with resistance between 600-1000℃ are ideal for high-temperature applications in industrial furnaces. For extreme environments exceeding 1000℃, coatings with resistance of 1000-2000℃ are utilized in specialized industries such as aerospace or metalworking.</p></p>
