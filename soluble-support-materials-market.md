<p><p>Soluble support materials market is categorized into three types based on water solubility strength. Strong water solubility materials dissolve quickly and completely in water, leaving no residue. Medium water solubility materials dissolve at a moderate rate, offering a balance between quick dissolution and support strength. Weak water solubility materials dissolve slowly, providing long-lasting support but may leave some residue. Each type caters to different needs in 3D printing processes, offering a range of options for manufacturers.</p></p>
