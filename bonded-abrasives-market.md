<p><p>Bonded abrasives are categorized into four main types: rubber bonded, glass bonded, resin bonded, and other varieties. Rubber bonded abrasives are ideal for precision grinding applications, while glass bonded abrasives are commonly used for cutting and polishing hard materials. Resin bonded abrasives are known for their high strength and durability, making them suitable for heavy-duty grinding tasks. Other types of bonded abrasives include ceramic, silicate, and metal bonded, each offering unique characteristics and advantages for specific grinding and cutting applications.</p></p>
