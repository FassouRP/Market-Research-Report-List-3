<p><strong>The Differential Pressure Flow Measurement Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Averaging Pitot Tubes</li><li>Nozzle Type Flow Meters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Differential pressure flow measurement systems consist of various types, including averaging pitot tubes and nozzle type flow meters. Averaging pitot tubes are inserted into the flow stream to measure the average velocity at various points, providing an accurate flow rate measurement. Nozzle type flow meters work by creating a pressure drop across a constriction in the flow stream, allowing for the calculation of flow rate based on the pressure difference. Both types of systems are commonly used in industrial and commercial applications for accurate flow measurement.</p></p>
