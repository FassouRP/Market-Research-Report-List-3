<p><p>Direct-coupling in EDI solutions refers to the direct exchange of electronic documents between two trading partners, without the need for a third-party network. This type of market is characterized by a more streamlined and cost-effective approach to EDI implementation. </p><p>On the other hand, the VAN market involves using a Value-Added Network (VAN) provider to facilitate the exchange of EDI documents between trading partners. This market type offers additional services such as data translation, security, and tracking, but may come with higher costs compared to direct-coupling.</p></p>
