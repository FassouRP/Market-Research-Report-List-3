<p><p>Oil-less Scroll Vacuum Pumps are divided into two main types: Single-sided Scroll and Double-sided Scroll. Single-sided Scroll pumps contain only one spiral scroll that moves in a circular motion to create a vacuum. Double-sided Scroll pumps have two spiral scrolls that work in tandem to produce a vacuum. Single-sided Scroll pumps are more compact and suitable for smaller applications, while Double-sided Scroll pumps are capable of generating higher vacuum levels and are typically used in larger industrial settings.</p></p>
