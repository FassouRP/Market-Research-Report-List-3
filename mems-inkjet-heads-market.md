<p><p>MEMS inkjet heads are divided into two main types: Continuous Inkjet and Random Inkjet. Continuous Inkjet heads are used for high-speed, continuous printing applications, where droplets of ink are continuously jetted onto the substrate. Random Inkjet heads are used for more precise and controlled printing applications, where droplets of ink are jetted onto the substrate in a random pattern. Both market types cater to different printing needs and are widely used in various industries for printing applications.</p></p>
