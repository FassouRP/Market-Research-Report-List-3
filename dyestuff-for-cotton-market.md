<p><p>Dyestuffs for cotton can be classified into different market types based on their chemical properties and applications. Reactive dyes form a covalent bond with cotton fibers, ensuring good color fastness. Disperse dyes are suitable for synthetic fibers and polyester blends. Direct dyes are used for coloring cotton directly without the need for a mordant. Sulfur dyes are commonly used for dark colors and denim. Optical brighteners are additives that enhance the whiteness and brightness of fabrics.</p></p>
