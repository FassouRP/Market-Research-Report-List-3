<p><ul><li>Fluorocarbon-based vacuum grease</li><li>Hydrocarbon-based vacuum grease</li><li>Silicone-based vacuum grease</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Vacuum grease is commonly classified into three main types based on their chemical compositions: fluorocarbon-based, hydrocarbon-based, and silicone-based. Fluorocarbon-based vacuum grease is known for its excellent chemical resistance and low vapor pressure, making it ideal for use in high vacuum applications. Hydrocarbon-based vacuum grease is often preferred for its compatibility with elastomers and plastics. Silicone-based vacuum grease is popular for its wide temperature range and resistance to oxidation. Each type of vacuum grease caters to specific needs and preferences in various industrial applications.</p></p>
