<p><p>Portable sound level meters are classified into two main types based on their accuracy and precision: ordinary sound level meters and precision sound level meters.</p><p>Ordinary sound level meters are designed for general use and are suitable for measuring noise levels in everyday environments. They provide reliable and accurate readings for most applications, such as monitoring noise levels in offices, factories, and public spaces.</p><p>In contrast, precision sound level meters are more advanced instruments that offer higher levels of accuracy and sensitivity. They are typically used in specialized applications where precise measurements are required, such as in scientific research, environmental monitoring, and quality control processes. Precision sound level meters are capable of detecting very low levels of noise and are equipped with advanced features to ensure accurate and consistent results.</p></p>
