<p><p>Solvent-based epoxy resin markets can be categorized into two types: direct process and indirect process. In the direct process, epoxy resins are directly dissolved in a solvent to form a stable solution, which is then used for various applications. In contrast, the indirect process involves the modification of epoxy resins through additional chemical reactions before being dissolved in solvents. Both processes have their own advantages and applications in industries such as construction, automotive, electronics, and coatings.</p></p>
