<p><p>Temperature Sensor Incorporating NTC Thermistor Market Types include Radial Type, Diode Type, Wire Bonding Type, Film Type, SMD Type, Wire Type, and Others. Radial Type sensors have the NTC thermistor enclosed in a metal or plastic case. Diode Type sensors use a diode to sense temperature changes. Wire Bonding Type sensors have the NTC thermistor wire bonded to a substrate. Film Type sensors use a thin film of NTC thermistor material. SMD Type sensors are surface-mount devices. Wire Type sensors use a wire-wound NTC thermistor element. Others include various other designs and configurations of temperature sensors.</p></p>
