<p><p>Military UAVs come in three main types in the market: Fixed wing, Rotary wing, and Hybrid. Fixed wing UAVs are designed to operate like traditional airplanes, offering longer endurance and higher speeds. Rotary wing UAVs, on the other hand, have the ability to take off and land vertically, making them more versatile for complex missions. Hybrids combine features of both fixed and rotary wings, offering a balance between endurance and flexibility for various military operations. Each type caters to specific mission requirements within the military UAV market.</p></p>
