<p><p>Surface Texture and Contour Measuring Instruments market includes Contact and Non-Contact types. Contact instruments, like stylus profilometers, use a physical probe to measure surface roughness and contour. Non-contact instruments, like optical profilometers and laser scanners, use light or laser technology to measure surface characteristics without touching the surface. Contact instruments may provide higher resolution but can cause wear and damage to delicate surfaces, while non-contact instruments are more suitable for fragile or sensitive materials. Each type has its own advantages and applications in various industries.</p></p>
